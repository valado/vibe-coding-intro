# Pricing Tiers

## Positioning Principle

Price to the value of *team velocity recovered*, not the hours delivered.
A team of 10 engineers spending 30% of their time on low-value AI cleanup is
losing ~€300,000/year in engineering capacity (at €100k average). This training
costs a fraction of that and recovers the gap in weeks.
