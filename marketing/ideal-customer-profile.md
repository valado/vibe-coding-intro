# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary ICP — Engineering-Led Scale-up

**Company size:** 50–500 employees  
**Stage:** Series A–C, or profitable bootstrapped  
**Industry:** SaaS, fintech, e-commerce, digital agency, consulting  
**Geography:** EU / DACH / US (English or German delivery)

### Characteristics
- Engineering team of 5–50 developers
- Already paying for AI tools (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Teams)
- Velocity is a competitive priority — time-to-ship matters
- No formal AI engineering methodology in place yet
- CTOs or VPs of Engineering frustrated by inconsistent results from AI tools
- Beginning to think about "agent-native" development but don't know where to start

### Pain Points
- Developers use AI differently — no consistency across the team
- AI-generated code ships with bugs that get caught in review (or production)
- Senior engineers spend time cleaning up AI output instead of shipping features
- The word "vibes" makes leadership nervous — no structured process
- Can't onboard new hires into the AI workflow systematically

### Trigger Events (buy signals)
- Just adopted a new AI coding tool org-wide
- Engineering manager role recently hired or promoted
- Post-funding: need to scale engineering productivity
- Competitor is visibly shipping faster
- Upcoming product launch with hard deadline

---

## Secondary ICP — Enterprise Digital Transformation

**Company size:** 500–5,000 employees  
**Buyer:** L&D department, CTO office, or Engineering Excellence team  
**Budget:** €5,000–€50,000 per engagement

### Characteristics
- Large engineering org with heterogeneous AI adoption (some teams use it, most don't)
- Internal training function that buys external specialized content
- Governance concerns around AI — needs structured, auditable methods
- Building an internal "AI Guild" or Center of Excellence

### Pain Points
- Inconsistent AI usage creates compliance and quality risks
- Senior engineers skeptical — "AI writes bad code"
- Need a curriculum that works for both experienced and junior developers
- Looking for external validation to build internal momentum

---

## Disqualifiers (Not a Good Fit)

- No engineering team (agency model, no internal dev)
- Team < 5 developers (individual coaching is a better fit)
- No existing AI tool spend — not yet ready to operationalize
- Looking for a chatbot or AI tool vendor, not training
- Purely non-technical teams (design-only, marketing-only)

---

## Decision Makers

| Role | Pain | Message |
|---|---|---|
| CTO / VP Eng | Inconsistent output, velocity risk | "Give your team a system, not just tools" |
| Engineering Manager | Review burden, junior AI usage | "Stop cleaning up AI code — train the pattern" |
| L&D / People Ops | No AI training curriculum | "Practitioner-led, production-tested curriculum" |
| Founder (early stage) | Need to ship fast, small team | "Compress months of trial-and-error into one day" |
