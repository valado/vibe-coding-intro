# From Vibes to Production
### Enterprise AI Engineering Training

---

## The Problem Your Teams Are Facing

AI tools are everywhere. But access ≠ capability.

Most engineers and product teams are using AI the same way they used Stack Overflow — copying outputs, hoping for the best, and shipping code they don't fully understand. The result: slower velocity, fragile systems, and a growing gap between what AI *could* do and what it *actually* does in your organization.

The bottleneck isn't the model. It's the method.

---

## What This Training Does

**"From Vibes to Production"** is a structured, practical training program that transforms how your teams work with AI — from ad-hoc experimentation to a disciplined, production-grade workflow.

Built by practitioners. Delivered in half-day or full-day workshop formats.

---

## What Your Teams Will Learn

| Module | Outcome |
|---|---|
| **The Agentic Mindset** | Shift from "asking AI questions" to "directing AI engineers" |
| **Context Architecture** | How to set up AI with product vision, guardrails, and tech stack alignment |
| **Specialist Agent Design** | Build focused AI roles (frontend, backend, QA, DevOps) for better results |
| **Stack Selection for AI** | Choose technologies where AI performs best — and why it matters |
| **Plan-First Engineering** | Use AI's architect mode before writing a single line of code |
| **Visual Verification** | Give AI eyes — browser automation, screenshot feedback loops |
| **Production Discipline** | Atomic commits, verification cycles, rollback strategies |
| **Scaling to Teams** | Prompt requests, agent handoffs, knowledge transfer patterns |

---

## The Result

Teams that complete this training ship **faster**, with **higher quality**, and with **fewer surprises** in production. More importantly, they have a repeatable system — not a bag of tricks.
